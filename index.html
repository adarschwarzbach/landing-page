Kyla Schwarzbach:
Fine Art and Graphic Design

Hi, my names Kyla Schwarzbach, I’m an eighteen year old artist from the Bay Area. I create art to connect with people and as a means of exploring the world around me. Currently, my body of work focuses on familiar themes; I explore the influence of light, experiment with self-portraits, and focus on emotions in portraiture.
